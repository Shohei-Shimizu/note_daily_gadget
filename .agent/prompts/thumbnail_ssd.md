# Thumbnail Generation Prompts for "External SSD"

## Common Style
- **Style**: Photorealistic lifestyle photography.
- **Lighting**: Soft natural light from a window (side lighting). High-key and bright.
- **Composition**: Depth of field with blurred background (boke). Main objects centered.
- **Color Palette**: White, Natural Wood, Black (ACCENT). Clean and minimal.

## Variation 1: MacBook Side Insertion
- **Subject**: A small, sleek black stick-type SSD plugged directly into the side USB-C port of a MacBook Pro.
- **Scene**: A wooden desk workspace.
- **Details**: The SSD is compact and cable-free. The laptop screen shows a file transfer progress bar (blurred/abstract).
- **Background**: A blurred bright living room with a green plant.

- **Details**: A USB-C cable connects the SSD to a laptop (out of frame or partially visible). Texture of the SSD is matte rubbery material.
- **Background**: Sunny window with sheer curtains.
